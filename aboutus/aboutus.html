About Us

Have you ever questioned why you're doing what you're doing? So did we! After several years working in various studios and traveling for shows, our love for the work came into question. What we found was it wasn't the work itself we no longer enjoyed, but the hectic scheduling.

That was when we started dreaming of a salon and spa that not only allowed our customers to relax, but kept us centered as well. That's how Pure Elegance was born, and let us tell you, we will never go back. Come and join us and let us be your sanctuary from the chaos of every day life.
